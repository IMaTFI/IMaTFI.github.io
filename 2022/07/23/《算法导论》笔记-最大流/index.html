基本的Ford-Fulkerson算法

FORD-FULKERSON(G,s,t)
    for each edge(u,v) in E
        (u,v).f = 0
    while there exists a path p from s to t in Gf
        cf(p) = min(cf(u,v):(u,v) in p)
        for each edge(u,v) in p
            if(u,v) in E
                (u,v).f = (u,v).f+cf(p)
            else (v,u).f=(v,u).f-cf(p)
